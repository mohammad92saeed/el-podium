This Privacy Policy explains how El Podium ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile application and services.

By using El Podium, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information

  • Phone number (required for all users)
  • Password (securely stored in encrypted form)
  • Social media account information (if you choose social login)
  • User ID and basic profile information

If your social media account does not provide a phone number, you will be required to enter one manually.

b. Usage and Activity Information

  • Live stream participation
  • Points earned and used
  • Daily roulette participation
  • Prizes won
  • Playing history
  • Redeem history

c. Technical Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version
  • IP address (for security and fraud prevention)
  • Log and diagnostic data

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Enable login via phone number, password, or social media
  • Provide access to live streams
  • Track points, rewards, and prize eligibility
  • Display playing and redeem history
  • Process prize redemptions
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Rewards, Points, and Prizes

  • Users can earn points through live streams and daily roulette
  • Points may be exchanged for prizes according to in-app rules
  • Playing and redeem history is stored for transparency
  • We reserve the right to review suspicious or abusive activity

4. Social Media Login

If you choose to log in using a social media account:

  • We may receive limited profile information as allowed by the platform
  • We do not post on your behalf without permission
  • A phone number is required if not provided by the platform

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share information only:

  • With trusted service providers necessary to operate the app
  • To comply with legal obligations or lawful requests
  • To protect the rights, safety, and security of users and El Podium
  • In the event of a merger, acquisition, or business transfer

6. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including encryption, secure servers, and access controls. However, no system is completely secure.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as the account remains active or as necessary to provide services and meet legal requirements.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data, or withdraw consent where applicable.

9. Children's Privacy

El Podium is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app and reflected by an updated date.
